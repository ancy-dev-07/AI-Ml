What is an Algorithm?

An Algorithm is a step-by-step procedure used to solve a problem.

Simple meaning:

üëâ Algorithm = How to solve a problem step by step

Real-life Algorithm (Making Tea):
  1. Boil water
  2. Add tea powder
  3. Add sugar
  4. Add milk
  5. Serve tea

Why is it called Algorithm?

The term Algorithm comes from the name of a mathematician, Al-Khwarizmi.
